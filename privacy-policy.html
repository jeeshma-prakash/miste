Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal information collected by MISTEO PTY LTD via the website located at https://misteo.co and its related websites.

What is "personal information"?

The Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) currently defines "personal information" as information or an opinion about an identified individual or one who is reasonably identifiable, whether true or not, and whether recorded in a material form or not.

If the information does not disclose your identity or enable it to be ascertained, it is generally not considered "personal information".

What information do we collect?

The kind of personal information we collect depends on how you use the website. This may include company contact details, your name, email address, or phone number.

How we collect your personal information

We collect personal information when you input it into the website. We also collect cookies, which help identify site usage and enhance your experience. Cookies generally do not personally identify you.

Purpose of collection

The information is collected to provide the best service experience. We disclose it only to service providers helping operate the website or to support staff in their usual duties.

By using our site, you consent to direct marketing material. This is only done if we collected the information from you and it's reasonably expected. All marketing includes a simple opt-out method. Sensitive personal data is not used in marketing.

Access and correction

Under Australian Privacy Principles 12 and 13, you may access and request correction of your personal information. Contact us below for such requests.

Complaint procedure

If you have concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us. Our Privacy Officer will review your complaint, and if valid, we’ll work with you to resolve it. If unsatisfied, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner.

Overseas transfer

Your data may be transferred to recipients in India. By using our site, you consent to this transfer, acknowledging that Australian privacy protections may not apply there.

GDPR

While the GDPR offers additional protections for EU users, it does not apply here as we do not target or monitor individuals in the EU.

How to contact us about privacy

If you have questions, need access to your data, or wish to file a complaint, contact us at: hello@misteo.co
