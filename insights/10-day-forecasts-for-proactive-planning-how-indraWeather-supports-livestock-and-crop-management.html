  1. Optimal Planting and Harvesting Times: Knowing when to plant and harvest is key to maximising crop yields. Using IndraWeather forecasts, farmers can align these tasks with favourable conditions, improving crop performance and reducing waste.
  2. Efficient Irrigation: Water usage is a critical aspect of sustainable farming. By monitoring 10-day rainfall forecasts, farmers can adjust irrigation schedules to save water, cutting costs and conserving resources.

Supporting Livestock Health and Productivity with Accurate Weather Prediction Services

Livestock, especially beef and dairy cattle, are sensitive to temperature fluctuations. Heat stress is a major concern, particularly in warm climates, and can lead to reduced milk production, slower weight gain, and even death. In cattle, the Heat Load Index (HLI) is an essential measure, combining temperature, humidity, and wind to indicate the level of heat stress animals may experience. IndraWeather’s 10-day forecasts empower livestock managers to manage these risks with a high degree of accuracy.

  1. Preventing Heat Stress: When IndraWeather’s forecast shows an upcoming temperature spike, livestock managers can prepare by providing additional water, shade, or ventilation. This is especially important for beef and dairy cattle, which are vulnerable to heat stress when HLI rises.
  2. Optimising Feeding Schedules: During hot weather, cattle tend to eat less, which can impact growth and milk production. With advance notice of heat waves, managers can adjust feeding times to cooler periods of the day, improving intake and overall health.
  3. Managing Water Resources: Just like crop farmers, livestock managers rely on effective water use. IndraWeather’s accurate predictions of temperature and humidity allow them to prepare for days when water needs may be higher.

Livestock insurance, especially parametric insurance, depends on accurate weather data to predict and manage payouts based on pre-defined triggers. With hyper-local data, insurance companies can tailor policies for more precise and timely coverage, building trust and satisfaction with policyholders.
